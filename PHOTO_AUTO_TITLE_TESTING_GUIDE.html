📋 Testing Steps

1 Navigate to Milestone Detail

Go to any project → Click milestone → Open "Photos" tab

2 Select Photo Type

Choose from: Progress, Issue, Inspection, Quality, Before, After, General

3 Click "Auto" Button

Click the cyan "Auto" button with sparkles icon ✨

4 Verify Generated Title

Check format: {type}-{projectId}-{date}-{time}-{seq}

5 Optional: Edit Title

You can still manually edit the title if needed

6 Upload Photos

Select photos and upload as usual
